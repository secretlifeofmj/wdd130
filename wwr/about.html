Our Story

Experience the Rapids

At Whitewater Rafting Co., we live for adventure! Since 2003, our mission has been to deliver unforgettable river experiences. Our trained guides are passionate about the rapids, safety, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2003, Whitewater Rafting Co. started with one raft and a dream. Over two decades later, we’ve grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the country, known for safety, excitement, and unforgettable river adventures.
