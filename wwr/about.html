Whitewater Rafting Co.

At Whitewater Rafting Co., we live for adventure! Since 2003, our mission has been to deliver unforgettable river experiences to thrill-seekers and families alike. Our trained guides are passionate about the rapids, safety, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2003, Whitewater Rafting Co. started with one raft and a dream. Today, we’re one of the most trusted rafting companies in the U.S., delivering safe, thrilling, and scenic river trips nationwide.
